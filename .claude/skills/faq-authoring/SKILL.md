# FAQ Authoring

## Purpose
Use this skill to create useful FAQ content that reduces friction and answers likely questions.

This skill helps:
- identify relevant questions
- draft concise answers
- address objections
- clarify practical details
- support trust and conversion

## When to use
Use this skill when:
- a page needs an FAQ section
- objections are recurring
- details need clarification
- support content is missing
- a service or product page needs more reassurance

## Workflow

### 1. Identify likely questions
Check:
- what the user may hesitate about
- what practical details are unclear
- what trust questions may arise
- what should be answered before contact or purchase

### 2. Prioritize the FAQ
Classify:
- must-answer questions
- nice-to-have clarifications
- questions that belong on another page

### 3. Draft answers
Write answers that are:
- concise
- direct
- useful
- reassuring when relevant
- consistent with the page goal

### 4. Review usefulness
Check:
- whether answers are too vague
- whether they repeat the page unnecessarily
- whether they create new confusion
- whether key objections remain unanswered

## Output format
Always structure the answer as:

## Objective
## Priority questions
## Draft FAQ
## Questions to exclude or move
## Risks or gaps
## Next content step

## Guardrails
- Answer real user questions, not invented filler
- Keep answers concise and concrete
- Avoid repeating the whole page in the FAQ
- Use the FAQ to reduce friction, not add noise
